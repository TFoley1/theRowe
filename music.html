Recommendations

Twin galaxies

Twin Galaxies is a Mathrock album produced by the band Delta Sleep. The album consists of 11 songs (on apple music), it has some elements of Posthardcore with the vocals at moments but is mostly focused on the instrumentals. It tells a story of person getting lost at sea. It's not exactly made clear how but they talk about seeing wrecks and sea monsters in several songs. This album is great in my opinion and deserves a 9/10 from me. Flows very well, keep the listener engaged and not bored. It's also very satisfying to listen to.
